# arc42 Chapter Summaries

## 1. Introduction and Goals
Describes the purpose of the system, its stakeholders, and the main goals it should achieve.

## 2. Constraints
Lists technical, organizational, and process constraints that limit the design.

## 3. Context and Scope
Defines the system boundary and shows how it interacts with users and external systems.

## 4. Solution Strategy
Summarizes the overall architectural approach and the key design decisions.

## 5. Building Block View
Describes the system structure by breaking it into components and their responsibilities.

## 6. Runtime View
Shows how components interact during execution for important scenarios.

## 7. Deployment View
Explains how the system is deployed and mapped to infrastructure.

## 8. Cross-cutting Concepts
Describes common concerns such as logging, error handling, and configuration.

## 9. Architecture Decisions
Documents important design decisions, including their rationale and consequences.

## 10. Quality Requirements
Defines quality attributes like performance, scalability, and maintainability.

## 11. Risks and Technical Debt
Identifies potential risks and areas of future improvement.

## 12. Glossary
Provides definitions of important domain and technical terms used in the system.
